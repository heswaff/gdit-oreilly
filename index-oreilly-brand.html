Case study

How Leidos turned upskilling into a strategic weapon.

Leidos needed to close skill gaps in crucial areas and prove ROI on learning investments. O'Reilly created a high-impact training program that retained top tech talent and unlocked millions in cost savings.

Challenges

  • High attrition risk among technical professionals.
  • Limited learning pathways in AI/ML and cybersecurity.
  • Difficulty justifying past training ROI.

Solution

  • Customized learning tracks mapped to critical business domains — AI, cyber, software development.
  • Seamless integration with internal systems (e.g., Workday).
  • Access to industry-leading expert content and live events.
  • Emerging: MCP integration into the engineering tool chain to meet developers in the flow of their work.
